Aims and Objectives of the platform

  • Interactive Environmental Education Platform ( IEEP) aims to overcome the issues which are inherent in the current system as mentioned above
  • To act as a supplement to the current system and encourage learners to take action.
  • Use freely available datasets on environment from United Nations and World Bank websites to create meaningful visual infographics which have an impact on young minds.
  • Create an online platform with an aim to get students outdoors.
  • To make it a global collaborative platform where learners can interact with other learners.
  • To make the project open source to facilitate future growth.
